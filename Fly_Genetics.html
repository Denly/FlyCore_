Fly Genetics

Using Drosophila as a model organism for genetic researches has been established for over a century. This list of readings provide basic information on Drosophila biology and genetics. Most of the readings will be covered by lectures of "Genetic Analyses in Model Organisms" or "Summer workshop on Drosophila genetics". A collection of online lectures on "The legacy of Drosophila Genetics" (http://hstalks.com/main/browse_talks.php?r=283&j=757&c=252) are great resources.
