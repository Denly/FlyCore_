Orientation

Using genetic or genomic approaches to address evolutionary questions is the most exciting research area in this field. This is the site for students who are interested in pursuing his/her graduate training in the Joint Evolutionary Genetics and Genomic Group at National Taiwan University and Academia Sinica. To us, a successful graduate training program should provide not only attractive research projects but training on the basic scientific skills. This site is established for providing a set of basic readings which are essential for young students towards the aim.

Getting Started

It is always good to start early and be well prepared for your future scientific career. A list of reading here is recommended for all undergraduate or perspective graduate students. Upon finishing all the required readings, you can move on to the essential reference list.
